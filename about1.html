About Our Library

  • Library at PeopleTree Education Society, Belagavi established in 2007. PTES Chairman and PeopleTree Education Society Principal have taken keen interest in establishing this Library to give the networked Library facility to the faculty members, staff and students, to get the best available information for research and reference. The Library working hours are from 09:00am to 06:00pm. This Library is totally 450Sq.m and area of Reading room 450sq.m. Latest Books in BBA,BCA,B.COM,PUC and General Books were also added for the reference of the needy staff and students. At present there are more than 5866 Books and 03 Journals in print versions, News Paper Online Journals are available. So daily more than 15 staffs and 100 students are making use of this Library regularly for retrieval of latest information.

    • LIBRARY SERVICES

  • One I.D. card for all services: The bar-coded library membership number as well as the number is embedded in the college identity card. Without this identity card he/she will not be entertained in the library at any circumstances because we are practicing ‘1 card for all services’.
  • Register: All the registered Students users are visiting register book their Entry in the Library.
  • Internet Browsing & Wi-Fi Facility: The free Internet and Wi-Fi service is available in the Library.
  • Subscribed e-Resources Packages: Library has subscribed Inflibnet and Databases to full fil information needs (especially e-Resources) of faculty and students
  • OPAC (Online Public Access Cataloging): A Digital facility to search Library catalogue to locate books, periodicals etc.
  • Safety & Security:
      1.Fire Extinguisher
      2. CCTV Camera Surveillance with Recording and Back-up Facility.
      3. Battery Facility for Uninterrupted power supply.
